Legal & Security

Privacy Policy

Please read our privacy policy carefully to understand how we protect your data.

1. Absolute Privacy & No Data Collection

We believe that your data belongs to you. HTML.COM.LY Publisher Portal does not collect, store, harvest, or share any personal information, usage statistics, or user-generated content. There are no databases, tracking scripts, or analytics tools running on our servers that monitor your activities.

2. Local-First Processing

All operations, including content preparation, formatting, and configuration, are executed entirely within your web browser. No files or text inputs are ever uploaded to our servers. This ensures complete confidentiality and offline capability.

3. Third-Party Links & Services

Our portal contains links to our main platform (a.html.com.ly). Once you navigate to external links, please refer to their respective privacy policies. We do not load external tracking cookies or third-party scripts on this portal.
